# [涉及句式 4] 核心考点：对...重要/有益/有害（注意 be of + 名词的用法）

1. 团队合作对孩子们的社交发展至关重要，而过度的屏幕时间对他们的身心健康是有害的。
> (提示：使用 vital/essential to 以及 harmful/detrimental to)
> Teamwork is essential for children to cultivate their social abilities, while long screen time is harmful to their physical and mental health.

2. 掌握一门外语对求职者来说具有巨大的价值，这也是现代教育不可或缺的一部分。
> (提示：使用 be of great value/importance to 以及 be integral to)
> Mastering a foreign language is of great importance for candidates, while it is integral to modern education.

# [涉及句式 5] 核心考点：构成威胁/带来挑战/施加压力（注意介词 to 和 on 的精准对应）

3. 严重的塑料污染不仅对海洋生态系统构成了巨大的威胁，还给当地的旅游业施加了沉重的压力。
> (提示：使用 pose a huge threat to 以及 put heavy pressure on)
> The plastic pollution not only poses a huge threat to ocean ecosystem, but also puts heavy pressure on the local tourism.

4. 人口老龄化给医疗保健系统带来了挑战，并给年轻一代强加了额外的经济负担。
> (提示：使用 present a challenge to 以及 impose an extra burden on)
> Aging people present a challenge to the healthcare system, while impose an extra economic burden on the juniors.

【句式 7】
目标句式：something has made it (difficult | easy | possible | convenient) for somebody to do something / provide somebody with opportunities to
1. [科技类]互联网的普及使得人们在家远程工作成为可能。（提示：使用 make it possible for）
> The popularization of the Internet has made it possible for people to work from home.

2. [教育类]国际交流项目为学生提供了体验不同文化和开阔眼界的机会。（提示：使用 provide... with opportunities to）
> International communication programs provide students with the opportunities to experience different culture and widen their horizen.

3. [城市/交通类]严重的交通拥堵使得通勤者很难准时到达工作岗位。（提示：使用 make it difficult for）
> Severe traffic jams make it difficult for the commuters to be on time.

目标句式：(consider it necessary | feel the need | find it (difficult | easy)) to do something / have difficulty in doing something

> A number of environmental activists consider it necessary to limit the usage of disposible plastic products.

> Modern parents sometimes find it difficult to balance their carrer development with accompanying their children.

6. [就业类]刚毕业的大学生在当前竞争激烈的就业市场中往往很难找到理想的工作。（提示：使用 have difficulty in doing）
> The graduates have difficulties in finding an ideal job in competitive job market.


【句式 9】
目标句式：somebody is (likely | unlikely | willing | unwilling | able | unable | reluctant | happy | delighted) to do something

> Comparing with the elder people, the youth are more reluctant to use traditional mindset.
