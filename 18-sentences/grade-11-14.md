### 【句式 11】表达重视与优先级

**1. A number of experts consider that schools should attach great importance to cultivate students' abilities of critical thinking.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **语法死角 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **绝对易错点：** `attach importance to` 这里的 `to` 是**介词**！后面必须接动名词。所以必须是 `to cultivating`。考官极其看重这种介词后接动名词的准确度。
    * **词汇地道性：** `abilities of critical thinking` 稍微有些中式，英语母语者习惯直接说 `critical thinking skills`。
* **修订版：** A number of experts consider that schools should attach great importance **to cultivating** students' **critical thinking skills**.

**2. The government should give priority to healthcare and public transport when allocating the budget.**
* **批改：** 🌟 **极佳 (Band 7.5)**
* **点评：** 完美！`when allocating the budget` 状语从句的省略形式用得非常高级，毫无语法瑕疵。如果在 `budget` 前加个 `national` (国家预算) 会更贴合原意。

**3. Modern enterprises tend to place a high value on collaboration awareness and communication skills.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **词汇升级 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **亮点：** 加了 `tend to` (倾向于)，这在雅思里叫 Hedging（柔化语气），是非常高级的学术写作习惯！
    * **中式英语：** `collaboration awareness` (合作意识) 偏中式直译。在英语里表达“团队合作精神”，最地道的是 `teamwork` 或 `team spirit`。
* **修订版：** Modern enterprises tend to place a high value on **teamwork** and communication skills.

### 【句式 12】利用资源与获得好处

**4. College students should make the most of online resources to complete their acdemic researches.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **拼写与单复数 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **拼写：** `acdemic` 漏了字母 a，应为 `academic`。
    * **词义偏差：** 原文是“补充(学术研究)”，不是“完成”，应使用 `supplement`。
    * **语法死角：** `research` 作为“研究”时是**不可数名词**，绝对不能加 `es`！这是中国考生最高频的扣分点之一。
* **修订版：** College students should make the most of online resources to **supplement** their **academic research**.

**5. Developing countries could reap the benifits of the blooming tourism to benifit local economy.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **拼写与重复 (Band 5.5 - 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **拼写错误：** `benifit` 是错的，应为 `benefit` (注意是 e 不是 i)。
    * **词汇辨析：** `blooming` 通常形容花朵盛开，形容经济或产业蓬勃发展必须用 **`booming`**。
    * **词汇重复：** 前面用了 `reap the benefits`，后面又用 `benefit` 作动词，句式显得重复。建议将后面的“促进”替换为 `boost` 或 `stimulate`。
    * **冠词与单复数：** 泛指当地经济，用 `their local economies` 或 `the local economy`。
* **修订版：** Developing countries could reap the **benefits** of the **booming** tourism **industry** to **boost the** local economy.

**6. To reduce the carbon footprint, we should take advantage of reusable powers such as solar energy and wind energy.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **专有名词不准 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **名词搭配：** “可再生能源”在雅思环境类是固定专有名词 **`renewable energy sources`**，不能自己生造出 `reusable powers` (听起来像可以反复使用的电池)。
    * **精简表达：** `solar energy and wind energy` 可以精简并列为 `solar and wind power/energy`。
* **修订版：** To reduce the carbon footprint, we should take advantage of **renewable energy sources** such as solar and wind **power**.

### 【句式 13】剖析因果关系

**7. Sitting long time and unhealthy eating habits sometimes cause the obesity problems in the young generation.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **词性与搭配 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **动词应用：** 题目提示了使用 `lead to` 或 `result in`，你用了 `cause`。尽量使用要求的词组来提升词汇多样性。
    * **主语结构：** `Sitting long time` 语法不规范。用动名词短语作主语应为 `Sitting for a long time`，但更地道的雅思满分词汇是 **`A sedentary lifestyle`** (久坐不动的生活方式)。
    * **冠词滥用：** 泛指肥胖问题，不需要加 `the`。
* **修订版：** **A sedentary lifestyle** and unhealthy eating habits sometimes **lead to / result in obesity problems among** the young generation.

**8. Industrailization and deforestation are responsible for the global warming and the climate change.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **拼写与冠词冗余 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **拼写：** `Industrailization` 应为 `Industrialization`。
    * **零冠词原则：** `global warming` 和 `climate change` 作为抽象的全球现象，前面**不需要**加 `the`。
* **修订版：** **Industrialization** and deforestation are responsible for **global warming** and **climate change**.

**9. According to researches, the rate of unemployment is related to the rise of the urban crimial rate.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **不可数名词与拼写 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **不可数名词：** 再次提醒，`research` 不可数！不能写 `researches`。可以用 `studies` 代替。
    * **拼写：** `crimial` 应为 `criminal`。
    * **细节缺失：** 题目是“密切相关”，漏翻译了“密切”(`closely`)。
    * **地道表达：** `the rate of unemployment` 直接说 `high unemployment rates` 更简洁。`urban criminal rate` 习惯说 `crime rates in urban areas`。
* **修订版：** According to **research/studies**, **high unemployment rates are closely** related to the rise **in crime rates in urban areas**.

### 【句式 14】采取行动与付出努力

**10. Governments should take emergent measures to deal with water pollution and protect marine ecosystem.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **词义混淆与冠词 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **致命错词：** `emergent` 的意思是“新兴的、初露头角的”(如 emergent markets 新兴市场)。“紧急的”必须用 **`urgent`** 或 `immediate`。
    * **单复数：** `ecosystem` 是可数名词，必须加冠词 `the marine ecosystem` 或变成复数 `marine ecosystems`。
    * **动词升级：** `deal with` 偏口语，雅思大作文写“应对/解决”强烈推荐 **`tackle`** 或 `address`。
* **修订版：** Governments should take **urgent** measures to **tackle** water pollution and protect marine **ecosystems**.

**11. Everyone should make an effort to protect traditional languages and customs from the attack from globalization.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **搭配偏中式 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * `the attack from globalization` 太过具象和中式了，全球化不会直接“攻击”人。表达抽象的负面影响，地道的说法是 `the impact of` 或 `the threat of`。
* **修订版：** Everyone should make an effort to protect traditional languages and customs from the **impact of** globalization.

**12. Parents should invest more time and energy in building firm link to their descendants.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **词汇感情色彩失调 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **亮点：** 你注意到了 `invest...in building` 的 `ing` 形式，这点非常棒，你已经吸收了前面纠正的介词考点！
    * **搭配不当：** `firm link` 通常用于建立网络链接或商业联系，人际间的情感联系用 `emotional bonds` 或 `strong relationships`。
    * **感情色彩失调：** `descendants` 是“子孙后代”，听起来像在看人类进化史或写遗嘱，极其生硬且有距离感。写家庭关系直接用 `children` 即可。
* **修订版：** Parents should invest more time and energy in building **strong emotional bonds with their children**.
