### 【句式 15】客观定性与特征描述

**1. Remote learning has been widely regarded as a effective replacement for traditional physical classes.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **基础语法失误 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **冠词错误：** `effective` 是元音发音开头，必须用 **`an effective`**。在雅思考试中，a/an 的低级错误极其影响考官的第一印象。
    * **词汇微调：** `replacement` 可以用，但雅思学术写作中更地道的词是 `alternative`（替代方案）。`physical classes` 意思懂了，但更标准的说法是 `classroom education/learning`。
* **修订版：** Remote learning is now widely regarded as **an** effective **alternative to** traditional **classroom education**.

**2. Carbon emission has been seen as the main reason of global warming.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **单复数与介词搭配 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **名词单复数：** 碳排放作为整体概念时，通常使用复数 **`Carbon emissions`**，因此谓语动词也要变成 `have been seen` 或 `are seen`。
    * **介词搭配：** `reason` 后面跟的介词通常是 **`for`**，而 `cause` 后面跟的是 **`of`**。所以你要么写 `the main reason for`，要么写 `the main cause of`。
* **修订版：** Carbon **emissions are** seen as the main **cause of** global warming.

**3. The old city is famous for its rich cultural heritages and acient buildings.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **不可数名词与拼写 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **拼写错误：** `acient` 漏了字母 n，应为 **`ancient`**。
    * **不可数名词雷区：** `heritage`（遗产）在这里是**不可数名词**，绝对不能加 `s`！
* **修订版：** The old city is famous for its rich cultural **heritage** and **ancient** buildings.

### 【句式 16】搭建段落骨架与亮明观点

**4. One major drawback of living in big cities is that the living expense is unaffordable.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **语气过于绝对 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **词汇搭配：** “生活成本”的固定搭配是 **`cost of living`**（首选）或者 `living expenses`（要用复数）。
    * **学术语气（Hedging）：** `unaffordable` 意思是“完全买不起/承担不起”。如果所有人都承担不起，那大城市就没人了。雅思写作忌讳把话说得太绝对，加上 `tends to be`（往往）或者用 `exorbitant`（过高的）会更加严谨。
* **修订版：** One major drawback of living in big cities is that the **cost of living tends to be exorbitant / high**.

**5. One significant benifit of using mobile phones is that people can contact with their family at any time any place.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **及物动词与拼写 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **拼写错误：** 再次提醒，是 **`benefit`**。
    * **动词用法：** `contact` 作为动词时是**及物动词**，后面直接加人，**不需要加 `with`**！这是极其典型的中式英语错误。如果要用 `with`，请换成 `keep in touch with`。
    * **表达精简：** `at any time any place` 稍微有点累赘，直接用副词短语 `anytime and anywhere`。
* **修订版：** One significant **benefit** of using mobile phones is that people can **contact their family anytime and anywhere**.

**6. My view is that only memorizing knowledge can not help students to achieve their academic success.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **书面语规范 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **词汇升级：** `only memorizing knowledge` 意思对，但学术词汇可以用 `relying solely on rote learning`（仅仅依赖死记硬背）。
    * **格式规范：** 在雅思正式写作中，`can not` 必须写成一个词 **`cannot`**，且不要用缩写（如 can't）。
    * **冠词冗余：** 泛指学术成功，不需要加 `their`。
* **修订版：** My view is that **relying solely on memorizing knowledge cannot** help students achieve academic success.

### 【句式 17】认知、理解与掌握

**7. Learning history makes students gain an insight into their ancestors' values and tradion.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **动词搭配与拼写 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **高级表达：** `makes students` 比较基础，学术英语更喜欢用 **`enables / allows students to...`**（使学生能够...）。
    * **拼写与单复数：** 拼写错误 `tradion`，应为 **`traditions`**。因为前面的 `values` 是复数，并列的传统也应该用复数。
* **修订版：** Learning history **enables** students **to** gain an insight into their ancestors' values and **traditions**.

**8. Outstanding managers should have a good grasp of the market trending and consumers' demand.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **地道搭配 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **名词词组：** `market trending` 不地道，趋势的名词就是 **`trends`**（market trends）。
    * **名词词组：** `consumers' demand` 语法没错，但英语母语者更习惯直接用复合名词 **`consumer demands`**。
* **修订版：** Outstanding managers should have a good grasp of market **trends** and **consumer demands**.

**9. Modern citizens should be well-informed about global problems, such as poverty and pollution.**
* **批改：** 🌟 **优秀 (Band 7.0)**
* **点评：** 这句话非常干净利落！唯一的小建议是，探讨全球宏观问题时，把 `problems` 换成 **`issues`** 会显得稍微正式一点点，但原句已经很棒了。
* **修订版：** Modern citizens should be well-informed about global **issues**, such as poverty and pollution.

### 【句式 18】引出观点与立场

**10. Someone argues that punishments which are more strict could warn potential crimers effectively.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **自造词语与逻辑主语 (Band 5.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **主语不当：** `Someone` 是单数，指“某个人”，雅思议论文讨论的是社会群体的观点，必须用 **`Some people argue`** 或直接用被动句。
    * **从句累赘：** `punishments which are more strict` 太啰嗦，直接写 **`stricter punishments`**。
    * **词汇搭配：** `warn` 是警告，“震慑犯罪”有一个雅思专属满分词汇叫 **`deter`**。
    * **自造词错误：** 英语里没有 `crimers` 这个词！罪犯是 **`criminals`** 或 **`offenders`**。
* **修订版：** **Some people argue** that **stricter** punishments could **effectively deter** potential **criminals**.

**11. The experts suggest that the government should invest more budget in disease preventing.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **词汇搭配 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **搭配不当：** `invest`（投资）后面接的是钱（money/funds）或者时间和精力（time/effort），不能直接投资“预算”。你可以说 **`allocate more budget to`**（分配更多预算给）或者 **`invest more funds in`**。
    * **词汇升级：** `disease preventing` 用法生硬，标准的医疗词汇是 **`disease prevention`** 或 `preventive healthcare`。
* **修订版：** Experts suggest that the government should **invest more funds** in disease **prevention**.

**12. Most of scientists agree that the artificial intellengence will affect our working in about tens of years.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **表达累赘与拼写 (Band 5.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **数量词搭配：** `Most of scientists` 是错的，要么写 `Most scientists`，要么写 `Most of the scientists`。
    * **拼写错误：** 人工智能是 `artificial intelligence`。前面不需要加 `the`，因为是抽象不可数名词。
    * **中式表达：** `affect our working` 表达太中式。改变“工作方式”应该说 `change the way we work`。
    * **中式表达：** `in about tens of years` 也是中文直译，英文里表达“未来几十年”的固定说法是 **`in the coming decades`**。
* **修订版：** **Most** scientists agree that artificial **intelligence** will **change the way we work in the coming decades**.
