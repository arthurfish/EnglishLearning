### 【句式 7】
**1. The popularization of the Internet has made it possible for people to work from home.**
* **批改：** 🌟 **非常好 (Band 7.0)**
* **点评：** 句式使用完全正确。`popularization` 可以用，但如果在雅思大作文中，使用 `The widespread use` 或 `The proliferation` (激增、扩散) 会显得更加学术和地道。

**2. International communication programs provide students with the opportunities to experience different culture and widen their horizen.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **需要微调 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **搭配升级：** “交流项目”一般叫 `exchange programs`，`communication` 偏向于语言或信息交流。
    * **单复数：** 泛指“不同文化”应使用复数 `different cultures`。另外，泛指机会，`opportunities` 前面的 `the` 可以去掉。
    * **拼写与搭配错误：** 拼写错误 `horizen` 应为 `horizons`。地道搭配是 **`broaden their horizons`**，极少说 `widen`。
* **修订版：** International **exchange** programs provide students with opportunities to experience different **cultures** and **broaden** their **horizons**.

**3. Severe traffic jams make it difficult for the commuters to be on time.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **良好，可升级词汇 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **词汇升级：** `traffic jams` 是口语词汇，雅思写作推荐用 **`traffic congestion`**（不可数）。
    * **冠词：** 泛指通勤者，不需要加 `the`，直接用 `commuters`。
    * **表达精准度：** `be on time` 稍微口语化，原文是“到达工作岗位”，翻译为 `arrive at work on time` 更贴切。
* **修订版：** Severe traffic **congestion makes** it difficult for commuters to **arrive at work** on time.

### 【句式 8】
**4. A number of environmental activists consider it necessary to limit the usage of disposible plastic products.**
* **批改：** 🌟 **非常棒，仅有拼写瑕疵 (Band 7.0)**
* **点评：** `A number of environmental activists` 用的非常地道！`consider it necessary to` 结构完美。
    * **拼写错误：** `disposible` 应为 **`disposable`**。
* **修订版：** A number of environmental activists consider it necessary to limit the usage of **disposable** plastic products.

**5. Modern parents sometimes find it difficult to balance their carrer development with accompanying their children.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **需注意中式英语 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **拼写错误：** `carrer` 应为 **`career`**。
    * **词意不准：** “经常”应翻译为 `often`，`sometimes` 是有时。
    * **中式英语预警：** `accompany`（陪伴）在英文中其实非常有距离感（比如保镖陪同、护士陪护）。表达父母“陪伴”孩子，最地道的说法是 **`spend time with their children`**。
    * **高分句型建议：** 建议使用雅思满分搭配 **`strike a balance between A and B`**。
* **修订版：** Modern parents **often** find it difficult to **strike a balance between** their **career** development **and spending time with** their children.

**6. The graduates have difficulties in finding an ideal job in competitive job market.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **注意名词单复数与冠词 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **固定搭配：** 在 `have difficulty (in) doing` 结构中，`difficulty` **通常作不可数名词**，不要加 `s`。
    * **细节补充：** “刚毕业的”最好翻译出来：`fresh graduates` 或 `recent graduates`。不要加 `the`（泛指）。
    * **冠词遗漏：** `competitive job market` 前面少了一个限定词，加上 `the current highly competitive job market` 会丰满很多。
* **修订版：** **Fresh** graduates have **difficulty** in finding an ideal job in **the current highly** competitive job market.

### 【句式 9】
**7. People keeping regular exercise and regular diet are unlikely to have long-term diseases.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **词汇搭配需升级 (Band 6.0)**
* **点评：**
    * **动词搭配：** 锻炼和饮食不搭配 `keep`。通常说 **`maintain regular exercise and a healthy diet`**。
    * **专有名词替换：** “慢性疾病”在雅思医疗健康类话题中有一个绝对核心词：**`chronic diseases`**（必须背下来，`long-term diseases` 显得太通俗）。“患上”常用 `suffer from`。
* **修订版：** People **maintaining** regular exercise and **a healthy** diet are unlikely to **suffer from chronic** diseases.

**8. Comparing with the elder people, the youth are more reluctant to use traditional mindset.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **注意语法死角 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **语法错误：** 作为状语，“相比于...”必须用被动形式的过去分词 **`Compared with/to`**。如果用 `Comparing`，意味着是年轻人在主动执行“比较”这个动作，逻辑不对。
    * **词汇升级：** `the elder people` 直接用 `the elderly` 或 `older generations` 更自然。`use mindset` 搭配别扭，通常用 `adopt / accept traditional ways of thinking`。
* **修订版：** **Compared** with the **elderly**, the youth are more reluctant to **accept** traditional **ways of thinking**.

**9. If companies provide flexible working time, the employees are more willing to work overtime for completing the task.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **介词使用需调整 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **词汇微调：** “工作时间”通常用复数 `working hours`。
    * **语法习惯：** 表目的时，英语更倾向于使用不定式 `to do` 而不是 `for doing`。所以 `for completing` 改为 **`to complete`**。
* **修订版：** If companies provide flexible working **hours**, employees are more willing to work overtime **to complete** the **projects/tasks**.

### 【句式 10】
**10. Home education plays a vital role in shaping children's characters and cultivating their good habits.**
* **批改：** 🌟 **完美 (Band 7.5)**
* **点评：** `shaping characters` 和 `cultivating habits` 这两个动宾搭配极其精准、地道！不需要修改。(`Family education` 也是很好的替换词)。

**11. Technology innovations hold the key to continuous development of economy.**
* **批改：** ✍️ **注意雅思高频核心词 (Band 6.5)**
* **点评：**
    * **词汇微调：** 科技创新一般用形容词+名词：`Technological innovation`。
    * **高分词替换：** 经济的“可持续发展”，不要用 `continuous`（连续不断，像水流一样），必须用雅思环境/经济类绝对核心词 **`sustainable`**。
* **修订版：** **Technological innovation holds** the key to **sustainable economic development**.

**12. Traditional festivals form an important part of national culture heritage, and thus should be protected well.**
* **批改：** 🌟 **极佳，细节微调 (Band 7.0)**
* **点评：** `and thus` 用的非常有逻辑感，加分！
    * **词性错误：** 文化遗产是 `cultural heritage`（形容词 `cultural`），不是 `culture`。
    * **副词位置/词汇升级：** `protected well` 可以用更学术的被动语态副词前置：**`be properly preserved`**（被妥善保护/留存）。
* **修订版：** Traditional festivals form an important part of national **cultural** heritage, and thus should be **properly preserved**.
