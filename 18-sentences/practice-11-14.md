【句式 11】表达重视与优先级
目标句式：somebody (lay emphasis | focus | place a high value) on something / somebody (give importance | pay closer attention | give priority | attach importance) to something
1. [教育类] 很多教育专家认为，学校应该更加重视培养学生的批判性思维能力。（提示：使用 attach importance to）
> A number of experts consider that schools should attach great importance to cultivate students' abilities of critical thinking.

2. [政府/民生类] 在分配国家预算时，政府应该优先考虑医疗保健和公共交通。（提示：使用 give priority to）
> The government should give priority to healthcare and public transport when allocating the budget.

3. [工作/企业类] 现代企业越来越看重求职者的团队合作精神和沟通技巧。（提示：使用 place a high value on）
> Modern enterprises tend to place a high value on collaboration awareness and communication skills.

【句式 12】利用资源与获得好处
目标句式：(make the most of | take advantage of | benefit from | reap benefits of | see the advantages of | make the optimal use of)...
4. [科技/学习类] 大学生应该充分利用在线资源来补充他们的学术研究。（提示：使用 make the most of 或 make the optimal use of）
> College students should make the most of online resources to complete their acdemic researches.

5. [经济/旅游类] 发展中国家可以从蓬勃发展的旅游业中获益，从而促进当地经济。（提示：使用 reap the benefits of）
> Developing countries could reap the benifits of the blooming tourism to benifit local economy.

6. [环境/能源类] 为了减少碳足迹，我们必须善加利用太阳能和风能等可再生能源。（提示：使用 take advantage of）
> To reduce the carbon footprint, we should take advantage of reusable powers such as solar energy and wind energy.

【句式 13】剖析因果关系
目标句式：(result in | lead to | cause | is the cause of | related to | contribute to | responsible for)...
7. [健康/生活方式类] 久坐不动的生活方式和不健康的饮食往往会导致年轻人的肥胖问题。（提示：使用 lead to 或 result in）
> Sitting long time and unhealthy eating habits sometimes cause the obesity problems in the young generation.
8. [环境/生态类] 工业化和过度砍伐森林是造成全球变暖和气候变化的主要原因。（提示：使用 contribute to 或 be responsible for）
> Industrailization and deforestation are responsible for the global warming and the climate change.
9. [社会/犯罪类] 研究表明，高失业率与城市地区犯罪率的上升密切相关。（提示：使用 be closely related to）
> According to researches, the rate of unemployment is related to the rise of the urban crimial rate. 

【句式 14】采取行动与付出努力
目标句式：take (steps | measures | action) to do something / make an effort to do something / (spend time | invest time and effort in) doing something
10. [环境/政府类] 各国政府必须采取紧急措施来应对水污染并保护海洋生态系统。（提示：使用 take measures to）
> Governments should take emergent measures to deal with water pollution and protect marine ecosystem.
11. [文化/全球化类] 每个人都应该努力保护传统的语言和习俗免受全球化的冲击。（提示：使用 make an effort to）
> Everyone should make an effort to protect traditional languages and customs from the attack from globalization.
12. [家庭/心理类] 父母应该投入更多的时间和精力与孩子们建立牢固的情感纽带。（提示：使用 invest time and effort in doing）
> Parents should invest more time and energy in building firm link to their descendants.
