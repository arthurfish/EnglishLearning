【句式 15】客观定性与特征描述
目标句式：be (seen | regarded | recognised | treated | taken | deemed | accepted) as / be (famous | well-known) for...
1. [科技/教育类] 远程学习现在被广泛认为是传统课堂教育的有效替代方案。（提示：使用 be widely regarded / recognised as）
> Remote learning has been widely regarded as a effective replacement for traditional physical classes. 

2. [环境类] 碳排放被许多科学家视为全球变暖的罪魁祸首(主要原因)。（提示：使用 be seen as / be deemed as）
> Carbon emission has been seen as the main reason of global warming.

3. [文化/旅游类] 这个古老的城市以其丰富的文化遗产和历史建筑而闻名。（提示：使用 be well-known / famous for）
> The old city is famous for its rich cultural heritages and acient buildings.

【句式 16】搭建段落骨架与亮明观点
目标句式：one (benefit | problem | advantage | disadvantage | drawback | reason | explanation) is that... / the downside is that... / my (view | opinion) is that...
4. [社会/城市类] 居住在大城市的一个主要缺点是生活成本往往过高。（提示：使用 One major drawback is that...）
> One major drawback of living in big cities is that the living expense is unaffordable.

5. [科技/生活类] 智能手机带来的一个显著好处是人们可以随时随地与家人保持联系。（提示：使用 One significant benefit is that...）
> One significant benifit of using mobile phones is that people can contact with their family at any time any place.


6. [个人观点类] 我的观点是，仅仅依靠死记硬背无法帮助学生取得真正的学术成功。（提示：使用 My view is that...）
> My view is that only memorizing knowledge can not help students to achieve their academic success.


【句式 17】认知、理解与掌握
目标句式：(gain an insight into | deepen their understanding of | have a good grasp of | learn more about | look closely at | gain an in-depth knowledge of | get familiar with | be aware of | be well-informed about)...
7. [教育/历史类] 学习历史使学生们能够深入了解他们祖先的价值观和传统。（提示：使用 gain an insight into）
> Learning history makes students gain an insight into their ancestors' values and tradion.

8. [工作/专业类] 优秀的管理者需要对市场趋势和消费者需求有很好的把握。（提示：使用 have a good grasp of）
> Outstanding managers should have a good grasp of the market trending and consumers' demand.

9. [社会/公民类] 现代公民应该对正在发生的全球性问题（如贫困和污染）保持充分的了解。（提示：使用 be well-informed about）
> Modern citizens should be well-informed about global problems, such as poverty and pollution.



【句式 18】引出观点与立场
目标句式：(believe | argue | accept | think | agree | suggest | realise | know | admit | find | understand) that...
10. [犯罪/法律类] 许多人认为（争论），更严厉的惩罚可以有效地震慑潜在的罪犯。（提示：使用 argue / believe that...）
> Someone argues that punishments which are more strict could warn potential crimers effectively.


11. [健康/医疗类] 专家们建议，政府应该在预防性医疗保健上投入更多资金。（提示：使用 suggest that...）
> The experts suggest that the government should invest more budget in disease preventing.


12. [科技/未来类] 绝大多数科学家都同意，人工智能将在未来几十年彻底改变我们的工作方式。（提示：使用 agree that...）
> Most of scientists agree that the artificial intellengence will affect our working in about tens of years.
