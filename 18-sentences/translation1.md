1. 因为科技的发展和人们财富的增加，人口寿命一直在延长。
2. 虽然老年人可以为社会做贡献，但是人口的变化可能会导致很多问题。
3. 一个和老龄化相关的问题是健康医疗的费用会激增，这会对纳税人施加负担。
4. 大部分的医疗需求和费用都在晚年出现。
5. 因此，政府需要花很多钱在照顾老年人上，而其他的一些关键服务如教育就会面临资金的问题。
6. 工作节奏慢可能是另外一个问题，随着年纪比较大的工作者数量增加。
7. 随着年龄增长，人们吸收信息和处理工作的能力下降。
8. 如果老年人推迟退休，会影响公司的效率和利润。
9. 另一方面，很多人会推迟退休并延长职业生涯，而他们的经验和智慧是种财富。
10. 人们既然更加长寿，他们会选择继续留在岗位上，而他们在自己的领域都是专家。
11. 他们可以作为顾问而工作并将自己的知识和技能传授给年轻人。
12. 比如，一些资深的管理人员会教年轻人如何管理一家公司，以及如何更好地服务客户。
13. 总而言之，寿命的急剧延长已经给很多国家的健康医疗施加了压力，并影响劳动力的产出。
14. 这会影响经济发展，虽然我们可以利用老年人的智慧。


As a result of the development of technology and the increase of the wealth, human's life has been prolonged in a long period. Althogh the senior citizens could contribute the society in some degree, there will be challenges arising in the trend.

One of a problem toward the elders' increasing is that the cost of health care would surge, which burdens tax-payers. A large portion of the demanding of health care presents in the older years. Therefore, the government has to spend huge amount of resource on the olders' care. Accordingly, other key public service such as education might suffer from a tigh budget.

The elders' low working pace might become another key problem, as the number of older worker roaring. Humans tend to be less efficient on obtaining information and handling their work, while their age being older. Nevertheless, if the elders pospone their retirements, there would be adverse effects on the companies' efficiency and income.

However, the trend also enables employees to have a longer career, pospones their retirement date, while their valuable experience and wisedom is a treasure for the company. When people live longer, they can stay on their position, and becoming experts on their tasks. For example, knowledgable managers can teach the juniors how to run an institution, and how to service their client better.

In general, the drastically extended life has imposed pressure on many contries' health and medical departments, while the workforces' outcome will be influced by the trend. Obviously, it affects the economy, while we still can take advantage of the seniors wisdom.
