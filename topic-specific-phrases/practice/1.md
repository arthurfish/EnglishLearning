# 学校的作用

- ❄ help children prepare the knowledge-based economy
  帮助学生们为知识经济做好准备
- ❄ help them develop their problem-solving skills
  增强他们解决问题的技能
- ❄ develop their independent thinking skills
  发展他们独立思考的技能
- ❄ help student develop good study habits
  帮助学生形成良好的学习习惯
- ❄ gain knowledge and practical experience
  获取知识和实践经验
- ❄ achieve their full learning potential 
  充分发挥出他们的学习潜力
- ❄ help them prepare for employment
  帮助他们准备就业

## 对比：

- ❄ improve their social skills 增强他们的社会交往技能
- ❄ develop their teamwork skills 发展他们的团队合作技能
- ❄ work closely together 一起紧密地合作
- ❄ develop a sense of responsibilities 形成责任感
- ❄ help them prepare for adult life
  帮助他们为成年之后的人生做准备
- ❄ become useful members of society 成为有用的社会成员
- ❄ become responsible members of society
  成为有责任感的社会成员


## 广泛学习多个科目还是集中学习少数科目

- ❄ broaden their knowledge and skills 拓宽他们的知识和技能
- ❄ develop a wide variety of habits 发展广泛的兴趣
- ❄ help them prepare for a wide range of careers. 帮助他们为多种职业做准备

### 对比：

- ❄ learn more efficiently 更高效地学习

# 教师在教育中的作用

- ❄  (?) are professional educators (教师) 是专业的教育工作者
- ❄ have more teaching experience  (老师比家长) 有更丰富的教学经验
- ❄ provide well-structed lessons  提供结构严谨的教学
- ❄ help students achieve their learning potential  帮助学生们充分发挥出他们的学习潜力
- ❄ help students develop social skills and teamwork skills  帮助学生们发展社会交往技能和团队合作技能

- ❄ have busy careers  (家长) 有忙碌的事业

## 科技对教学的作用

- ❄ make lessons more interesting and enjoyable  让授课变得更有趣而且令人愉快
- ❄ provide a variety of information 提供多种多样的信息
- ❄ help stduents find information quickly and easily  帮助学生快速而且轻松地找到信息
- ❄ can be used to improve lessons  可以被用来改进教学

### 对比：

# 越来越多的人独自生活的原因

- ❄ want to be more independent 希望更加独立
- ❄ want to enjoy more privacy 希望享受更多的隐私
- ❄ have higher income 有更高的收入
- ❄ could afford living alone 可以承担独自生活所需的费用
- ❄ keep in contact with their family by the Internet and moblie phones 用互联网和手机与家人保持联系

## 独自生活的好处：

- ❄ have more freedom to choose their personal lifestyles 有更多的自由去选择自己的生活方式
- ❄ increase their independence 增加他们的独立性

## 对比：

- ❄ rely heavily on social media 严重依赖于社交媒体
- ❄ reduce face-to-face communication 减少面对面的交流
- ❄ feel lonely and isolated 感觉孤独而且孤立

# 科技让人们可以选择到不同地方生活的利弊

- ❄ more educational chances 更多的教育机会
- ❄ more working opportunities  更多的工作机会
- ❄ achieve their full potential 充分发挥出他们的潜力
- ❄ experience other culture and lifestyles 体验其它的文化和生活方式


### 对比：

- ❄ lack a sense of belonging 缺少归属感
- ❄ feel lonely and isolated 感到孤独而且孤立
